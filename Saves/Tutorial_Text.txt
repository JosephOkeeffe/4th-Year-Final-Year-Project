Types of units

Fighters: are used to attack enemies and destroy enemies base
Workers: get assigned to buildings to gather resources

[Title]
Types of enemy
[Text]
There are two types of enemies: Suckler and Big Suckler
Sucklers have a male and female type
The females move towards the males and merge with them
This creates a Big Suckler who has more health 
[Texture Name]
suckler

[Title]
Enemy Bases
[Text]
The enemy bases are what you need to destroy in order to win the game
Each base has a different item requirement
Once the player has all the items required, it can unlock that base
Then the player will attack the base and destroy it
Once they are all destroyed the game is over and you win
[Texture Name]
enemy-base

[Title]
Spaceships
[Text]
Spaceships are what spawns more enemies in the game
They spawn at the edge of the map at set intervals
They drop eggs randomly and after a while these eggs hatch into Sucklers
There are 5 different types of spaceships that can spawn
The higher the tier, the more eggs they can spawn at a time
The amount of spaceships increases every interval
